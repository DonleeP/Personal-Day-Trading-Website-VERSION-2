About Me.

Who I am as a person and why I am making this website.

Donlee Pedersen
Computer Science student at UWGB

 I’m passionate about financial freedom, smart decision-making, and helping people take control of their future through day trading. I created this site not just to share strategies, but to build a space where beginners can learn the ropes without all the fluff. When I’m not analyzing charts or exploring new trading tactics, I’m probably studying, leveling up my skills, or finding ways to simplify complex ideas for others. Whether you’re just curious or seriously diving into trading, I’m glad you’re here.

I didn’t grow up knowing anything about the stock market. Like a lot of people, I stumbled into it out of curiosity and the desire to build something for myself. At first, it was overwhelming—charts, terms, risks—but the more I learned, the more it clicked. What started as a hobby became a passion, and now I’m committed to helping others cut through the confusion and start their own journey with confidence. This site is my way of giving back what I wish I had when I started.
